Hi and welcome to omegaUp

We are glad that you are here! This page serves as a reference for new volunteers, full time employees and trustees, it complements the New Volunteer Orientations which takes place on the first Wednesday of every month.

omegaUp is 501(c)(3) non-profit registered in the state of Washington since 7/12/2016. The omegaUp project itself started way back in 2011 but it was until 2016 that we became an organization. One of the most important parts of being an incorporated organization is writing the by-laws. The provisions contained in this document contain information central to the organization, they provide a practical yet formal set of rules for the orderly operation.
